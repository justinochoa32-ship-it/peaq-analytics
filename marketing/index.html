Athlete Profile

Make the profile instantly understandable.

PEAQ turns testing data into a profile coaches can explain quickly: what the athlete is, what is limiting them, and what to prioritize next.

  • Overall score, star rating, archetype, and status
  • Primary limiter, secondary limiter, and current strengths
  • Coach summary and training focus for better communication
Metric Scoring

Keep the details available without making coaches dig.

Coaches get the simple athlete story first, then the metric-level detail when they need the why behind the profile.

  • Metric snapshots for sprint, COD, jump, mRSI, and relative strength
  • Category buckets for athletic expression, power, strength, and efficiency
  • Clear low, moderate, and high scoring language for faster decisions
